Company Profile

Foshan City POKMEN INDUSTRIAL EQUIPMENT CO.LTD is a mechanical and electrical equipment company which integrates trade, technology and service. It mainly manages foreign mature automation equipment to fill the gap in China. The agency brands include: AND&OR, P&S, ACMI, NORTAN, BAUMER, CRYOTHERM, TECHNIFAB, DOHMEYER.

Since its establishment in 2012, its business scope has been expanding.

The company serves beer, condiment, Cereals, oils, foodstuffs, packaging machinery and so on.

The company has a large number of successful cases, including: gift packaging automation solutions, ultra high speed empty bottle testing and packaging solutions, promotional packaging automation solutions, high speed handle bottle leakage and handle assembly solutions, liquid nitrogen solutions for oil and fat, solution of high speed liquid nitrogen for beverages, solution of sterile liquid nitrogen High efficiency liquid nitrogen cooling tunnel solution, efficient energy saving blow drying system, low cost packaging solution, commercial cold sterilization solution (PEF pulse electric field sterilization, AC high electric field sterilization).

After years of accumulation, the company has established a cooperative relationship with advanced foreign automation companies to bring a reliable and mature solution for the Chinese customers. It returns the rapid application automation of the industry leading enterprises and brings a new extreme experience.
